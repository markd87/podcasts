Podcasts Transcriptions

Podcasts contain a lot of useful raw information that smart and interesting people share during a live unrehearsed conversation. To be able to go back and remind myself about some of the topics or wisdom that was shared, having the transcriptions of these conversations can be handy. Here I will put transcription of my favorite podcasts.
Transcripts are generated using OpenAI's Whisper model in the following Google Colab notebook and then converted to html with the timestamps linked to the youtube video of the podcast.
